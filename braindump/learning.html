Learning

  • Some tips on being smart (Source).

    TLDR: Read to improve skills and knowledge and practice while reading.

    • Read
      • School and College don't necessarily make you samrt. Reading make you smart.
    • What to read? Non Fiction
      • You can read Fiction to improve vocab, and reading speed
      • Which books? Search for books on topics you want to learn.
    • How to read?
      • Practice: Practicing the skill makes your brain think it is important and remembers it. E.g. Test in school are way to make you think the information is necessary and make you remember it.
      • Imagine: If you can't practice, imagine situation where you need the skill. And practice in your imagination.
    • Do practice questions.
    • Books are best.
  • Learning is not supposed to be fun. It doesn't need to be boring either. Give yourself a real workout. Shortification of Learning
