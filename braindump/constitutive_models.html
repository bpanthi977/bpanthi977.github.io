Constitutive Models

(from abstract of https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/B9780124077164000065)

  • Stress-Strain Relation: Constitutive models describe the material responses to different mechanical and/or thermal loading conditions.
  • Part of Governing Equations: together with the conservation laws and kinematic relations, stress-strain relations formulate the governing equations of the material

Components of Constitutive Models:

  • EOSs (Equation of States): Relate pressue to volume & internal energy/temperature
  • Strength models: Relate the deviatoric stress to deviatoric strain.
  • In addition, a failure criterion is required to identify the onset and describe the evolution of material failure.
