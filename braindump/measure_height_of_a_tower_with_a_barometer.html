Measure height of a tower with a Barometer

A Neil's Bohr Story

In an exam, there was a question: "There is a tower. How would you measure the height of the tower using a Barometer?".

Bohr said:

  • I could measure the pressure at the bottom, and at the top, and work out the height
  • Throw the barometer from the top of the tower, and time the fall, …
  • I could knock on the door, and ask the person to tell me the height of the tower in exchange for a nice Barometer.

There are many methods to solve a problem.
