  • Getting Started: https://www.lesswrong.com/about

LessWrong is an online forum and community dedicated to improving human reasoning and decision-making. Rationality.

LessWrong and its community has strong interest in Artificial Intelligence. LessWrong was seeded by the writings of Eliezer Yudkowsky, an artificial intelligence researcher.
