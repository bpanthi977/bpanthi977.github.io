1. Many Processes in Chemistry Are Acid-Base Reactions

Common acids and bases include aspirin (acetylsalicylic acid) and milk of magnesia (magnesium hydroxide). Besides providing the basis of many medicinal and house- hold products, though, acid-base chemistry is important in industrial processes and essential in sustaining biological systems.
