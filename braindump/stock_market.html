1. Primary Market

new stocks to the public are offered for the first time directly through public

  • IPO (Initial Public Offer) Rs. 100 ma euta share
  • FPO (Follow on Public Offer) Company ramro huda gayo bhane e.g. chilime le Rs. 400 ma becheko thiyo
  • Book Building/bidding (Offering through bids; securities board of nepal just issued this type of sytem in Nepal) => Ramro companies haru ko stock 100 ma dida company lai ghata hunxa, so bidding garera surumai high value ma bechxan.

Book Building naya company lai ho FPO purano company (IPO gari sakeko) lai bhanxa.

4.1. Fundamental Analysis

  • EPS < 1 => company faida ma xaina aile kunai kunai hydropower ko EPS -ve ma pugeko xa
  • P/E jati high teti overvalued
  • Book value dherai bhako ramro
  • Dividend history => kati share kati cash dinxa e.g. Uniliver 1000% dividend dinxa. ekdam ramro
  • Net worth per share
  • Non performing load (NPL) of BFI (bank or financial institution) kati loan clear garna sakexainan
  • Board of Members of Company e.g. NEA Kulman le garda faida ma xa
