Scheduling Your Week

  • The 3 Part Split
    • Your energy levels keep on decreasing and your being tempted level keeps increasing
    • so keep high energy high temptation tasks in the morning,
    • high energy low temptation in the afternoons
    • low energy low temptation in the evening
  • The Mission Impossible
    • If there is a big project, keep note in your calender to remind you of that task, even if you can't complete it
    • This will open a daemon that would think about it in the background
  • The PR Rule : Work expands to fill the time
  • Morning Glory
    • Start your day with something that you are looking forward to
  • The Fun Factor : Separate time for socializing, fun stuff, …
  • Strategic Overscheduling
    • write tasks as you think of them in the calender (no need of todo list)
    • so, you won't forget, and it acts as remainder, and you may do it or reschedule it
