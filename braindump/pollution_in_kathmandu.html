"Pollution in Kathmandu in Engineering Perspective"

1. Some facts

  • 5th in pollution index
  • ~1000 people die due to air polution reated causes
  • Durinal Behaviour

2. Causes

  • Brick Kilns (120 kilns) contribute ~11% of PM 10
  • lact of grenary min 10% but only 4% available
  • increasing vehicles (at 14% per annum)
  • hapazard urbanization; Urban Heat Island
  • underground water depletion and lacking treatmnet plants
    • water pollution
    • soil pollution

3. Acts and Programmes by Governments

  • Water resource act 2049 BS
  • the aquatic animal protection act 2017 BS
  • Envinronment protection act 1996
  • kathmandu valley developement authority act 2045 BS
  • water management Policy 2066 BS

4. Solutions

4.1. Ecofriendly constructions

  • minimize cutting and filling
  • construction material manufacturing companies away from valley
  • temporary alternative routes during road construction

4.2. Old Vechicles management

  • Encourage programs like green stickers
  • exhaust treatment technologies (e.g. DPF)
  • fuel quality and prohibit adulteration
  • quality of road

4.3. Sewage line construciont / river water purification

  • Bank stabilization
  • flow modification
  • treatement should be done which reduces sluge produced which then is easier to manage

4.4. Waste management/dumping area construction

  • reduce waste generation
  • improve collection service
  • utilize the funds properly
  • proper dumping sites and land filing sites
