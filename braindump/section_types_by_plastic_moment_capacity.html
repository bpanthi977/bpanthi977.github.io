Section Types by Plastic Moment Capacity

Table of Contents

  • 1. Plastic Section
  • 2. Compact Section
  • 3. Semi-Compact Section

See Steel Structure Design Standard for more details.

1. Plastic Section

Cross-section, which can develop a plastic hinge and sustain plastic moment over sufficient plastic rotation required for formation of plastic failure mechanism of the member or structure.

2. Compact Section

A cross-section, which can develop plastic moment but has inadequate plastic rotation capacity needed for formation of a plastic collapse mechanism of the member or structure.
