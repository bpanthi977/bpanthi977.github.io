Diving Reflex

Cold water triggers the trigeminal nerve, to send signal to brain stem to trigger survival response:

  • our heart go slower (bradicardia),
  • oxygen is increased to brain and critical organs
  • spleen contracts to increase the RBC count in blood

We can exploit the diving reflex to make ourselves calm. Just wash your facex with cold water when you are nervous.
