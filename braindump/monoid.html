Monoid

  • A set equiped with a binary opertor that
    • is associative
    • has an identity element
  • A Semi Group with an identity element.
  • A monoid which is commutative is an commutative monoid or abelian monoid.

Examples:

  • non negative integers with additions
  • positive integers with multiplication
