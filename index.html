Distributing chocolates to children

In how many ways can you distribute n chocolates to m children? Simplifying the condition If we consider all chocolates to be different/unique (and of course children are also unique :) ), then the first chocolate can go to any one of the n children and the next one too can go to any one of the n children and so on.
